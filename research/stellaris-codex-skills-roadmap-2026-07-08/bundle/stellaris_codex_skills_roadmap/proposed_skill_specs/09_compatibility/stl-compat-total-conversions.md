# stl-compat-total-conversions

> Roadmap card only. This is not a finished Codex skill and is not a `SKILL.md` body.

## Purpose

Keep total conversions isolated and check version, UI, resource, and compatibility statements before mixing with vanilla+ stacks.

## Trigger

STNC, STNH, LoToR, New Dawn, or another total conversion is considered.

## Do not use when

Normal vanilla+ compatibility patching with no total conversion.
