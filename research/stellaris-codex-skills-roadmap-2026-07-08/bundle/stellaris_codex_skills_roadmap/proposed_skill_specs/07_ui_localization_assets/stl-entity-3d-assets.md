> Roadmap card only. This is not a finished Codex skill and is not a `SKILL.md` body.

Validate entity, mesh, animation, shader, and graphical culture references for shipsets, portraits, and space objects.

## Trigger

3D models, shipsets, entity errors, or graphical culture assets are touched.

## Do not use when

## Likely source references or evidence types

- vanilla entities
- mesh paths
- asset selector files
- error.log
- render screenshots
