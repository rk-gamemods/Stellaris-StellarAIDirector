> Roadmap card only. This is not a finished Codex skill and is not a `SKILL.md` body.

## Purpose

Assess high-power megastructure effects, crises, menus, resources, scripted triggers, vanilla overwrites, and save/runtime risks.

## Trigger

Gigas or Gigas-like megastructure content is present, or megastructure changes are high-power.

## Do not use when

Small vanilla megastructure text or cost tweak.

## Likely source references or evidence types

- Gigas files/pages
- mod_maintainer_guidance_matrix.csv
- Irony conflicts
- runtime logs
- UIOD/URP patches
