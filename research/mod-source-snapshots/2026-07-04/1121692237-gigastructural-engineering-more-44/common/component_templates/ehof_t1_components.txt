#####################################################
##### SENTIENT METAL / NEGATIVE MASS - "tier 1" #####
#####################################################
#Design guide: vanilla ~T6ish equivalent but with T7 regen for shields, Galvanic
# regen for armour, boosted tracking (weapons) and power (reactors), to give
# the tier a lore-fitting unique mechanical identity. So about tier 6.5 total.

#For the purposes of easily referencing component costs/upkeeps to vanilla,
# giga_sr_negative_mass should be a rare resource slightly more valuable
# than gases/motes/crystals
