## Quality Gate Correction

The first pass overweighted content keywords and underweighted collection quality. That was wrong for choosing a playset base.

For collection recommendations, apply these pre-filters before content matching:

- current target version support, currently Stellaris 4.4.x;
- recent update or revision after the target game patch;
- meaningful ratings, favorites, subscribers, visitors, or comments;
- visible load-order guidance or installation instructions;
- active creator maintenance signals;
- no obvious `Not enough ratings`, stale, `waiting for update`, removed, incompatible, or reported-problem signals.

Using those filters, `Stellaris - Best Mods - 4.0` (`2866396804`) should not have been promoted despite its attractive mod list. The public page shows `Not enough ratings`, warning text, and it includes `Real Space 4.0`. Likewise, `NSC3 + Gigastructures` (`3214357045`), `Stellaris - Realistic, Challenging and Customizable` (`3533545786`), and `4.4 + waiting for update` (`3501610395`) should be treated as idea sources only, not base recommendations, because they fail adoption/freshness/warning/noise filters.

## Trait And Immortality Notes

### Additional Traits

Steam ID: `681576508`

Good fit for the user's broad species-trait preference. The Workshop page says it was updated for 4.4 and adds many organic, presapient, and robotic advantages/handicaps. It is popular and directly about more diverse galaxy traits.

Compatibility risk: its page says it overwrites multiple vanilla species-trait files. Use Irony before combining it with other species-trait mods such as `Just More Traits`, `Species Traits Expansion: Galactic Diversity`, or overhaul ethics/civics mods.

### More Leader Traits

Steam ID: `3195070547`

Good fit for leader variety. The page says it adds 46 leader traits and has different flavor text for normal, hive, and machine empires.

Compatibility note: it currently leans on Galactic Paragons for most traits.

### Chosen Messiah Trait

Steam ID: `2875397266`

Best direct match found for the specific "immortal ruler / elf empire / thematic immortal leader" idea. The page describes a species trait that grants the current ruler the Chosen One trait, has variants for pre-designed AI empires, and comments from 2026-06-28/29 mention 4.4 and a Gigastructural test with no observed bug.

Compatibility risk: the page says it overrides the vanilla `civic_psionic_sovereign` civic after later updates. This should be checked with Irony if using other psionic, civic, ethics, or ascension mods.

### Just More Traits 4.x

Steam ID: `3703910404`

Potential trait source from the giant `4.4 + waiting for update` menu. It includes traits such as longer leader lifespan, but it is much lower-signal than `Additional Traits`: fewer subscribers, no clear 4.4-specific page text found in the first pass, and likely overlaps with other trait-file edits.

## Practical Starter Trim

Start from `2473560875`, then remove or hold:

- Star Wars themed shipsets and patches;
- unrelated shipsets and portrait packs unless intentionally wanted;
- overlapping UI submods until readability is checked;
- any trait mod that overlaps with another trait-file overwrite until Irony shows the conflict;
- any performance/AI mod until the base game starts and saves cleanly.

Then consider adding, one at a time:

- `Chosen Messiah Trait` for immortal-ruler themed empires.
- `Additional Traits` if not already present in the chosen base and if Irony conflict review is acceptable.
- `Stellar AI` from the previous best collection if AI competence matters more than performance-only tweaks.
- `Starbase Extended 3.0` or `Starbase Expansion` for stronger static defenses.

## Remaining Verification

- Use Irony to inspect conflicts among trait mods before combining `Additional Traits`, `Species Traits Expansion: Galactic Diversity`, `Just More Traits`, `More Leader Traits`, and ethics/civics overhauls.
- Check whether the selected trait mods expose options to AI-generated empires or only player/pre-designed empires.
- Test `Chosen Messiah Trait` with Gigas, Psionic Species Expansion, and any ethics/civics overhaul before making it part of the default playset.
- Verify live Workshop comments immediately before subscribing, because this research was performed during the active 4.4.4/4.4.5 update window.
